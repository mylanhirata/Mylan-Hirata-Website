Mylan Hirata

Professional Golfer

My name is Mylan Hirata and I am a professional golfer. I began golf at age 6 by taking lessons at Mile Square golf course in Fountain Valley, CA with my aunt Gail Chafen-Hirata. My aunt was a professional golfer in the 80s and she wanted to start training me at a young age. I played high school golf for two years at Fairmont Preparatory Academy and two years at Troy high school. During my junior year of high school, my team were the California state CIF runner ups and senior year we were state champions. During my high school years I also played Toyota Tour Cup and AJGA tournaments outside of school. I then went on to play golf for the University of California Riverside for the next four years and majored in business with a concentration in management.
